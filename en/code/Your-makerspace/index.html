Did you know?


The office makerspace belongs to you!

What is a makerspace?

A makerspace is a designated room for creation, innovation, iteration and destruction.


If you're really lucky (turns out, you are!) your makerspace contains some really cool tools and materials to make nearly anything! Not all makerspaces have fancy tools, you just need a safe designated place to make some things (and pull others apart!)

"Imagination is more important than knowledge. Knowledge is limited. Imagination encircles the world."

- Albert Einstein.

Why have a makerspace?


We have a makerspace so that all of our team have somewhere they can:

  • Improve their Digital Making skills to better understand and live the mission of the Raspberry Pi Foundation
  • Receive engaging and enjoyable professional development in the controlled environment of the office
  • Have somewhere to create, innovate, make, destroy, connect, build, repair, design and invent!

Get Involved!

You can easily get involved - message MrC on Slack to organise your 15 minute safety induction for the next time you're in the office!



We can also run inductions for your team alongside a fun Digital Making activity for your team day - just ask!
