Make some Vegan Banana Bread

Follow my recipe for a tasty vegan snack.

ğŸŒ

Ingredients

  • 4 small bananas
  • 75 grams of vegetable oil
  • 100 grams of caster sugar
  • 225 grams of plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius
  2. Mash bananas with a fork
  3. Mix mashed bananas with vegetable oil
  4. Add sugar, plain flour and baking powder and mix well
  5. Optional: Add nutmeg and mix well
  6. Pour mixture into a lined loaf tin
  7. Bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through
